Our Story

EVARA is more than just a name— it’s a reflection of our passion for storytelling. Inspired by “Ever”, it symbolizes timeless moments, while “Aura” represents the beauty and emotions we capture in every frame. It resembles words like “Avara” (अवर्त) or “Eshvara” (ईश्वर), which relate to greatness, divinity, or protection.
